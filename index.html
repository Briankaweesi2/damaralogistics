Get To Know Us.

Damara Logistics is a privately held and independent clearing and forwarding company offering total logistics solutions to individuals and companies.

General Cargo Transportation

We offer a range of transportation services, both international and domestic retail delivery of goods by land, guaranteeing the satisfaction of our customers, with cost-effective, reliable and on-time delivery solutions.

Clearing and Forwarding

Being the leading customs clearing agents our expert team do a proper study of all local rules and regulations so that they can help you overcome even the most complex matters of trade compliance.

Warehousing

We have both both bonded and non bonded ware houses in Mukono, Wakiso and Kampala. Our locations are in close proximity to the City. That helps us in providing cost effective warehousing services to clients.

Reassuring Customers

Our Customs Brokers help ease Import and Export regulations and paperwork in record time for all of your shipments. Handling all the trade compliance and procedures, we help clear consignments by sea, land and air more efficiently.

Safe and Secure

Our warehouses have proper ventilation and temperature controlled facilities that help us in effective storage of cargo till final dispatch. All cargo handling services conform to the international quality standards.

Flexibility and Transparency

All aspects of the transportation process, including information exchange, management and operations are effectively and efficiently linked to offer our customers total value, flexibility and transparency.

Get In Touch.

We understand diverse Customs regulations and we are under special arrangements with URA, which means for our customers – shipments are cleared quickly and efficiently.
